Local weather in Hämeenlinna

January gets a dramatic decision this year. A snowstorm called Valtter will clutter traffic in the southern and central parts of the country throughout the weekend, and conditions in Hämeenlinna in particular can be so difficult that it is worth avoiding traffic by all means. During the weekend, the snow reaches more than 30 cm in some places and snowdrifts can grow closer to a meter due to the wind. Crown snow-load damage also seems likely.
